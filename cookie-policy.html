COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: January 30, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are commonly used to ensure websites function properly and to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

Nova Hub uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as saving theme and site settings
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices like starred games and customization options
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site and improve performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.

Persistent Cookies: Stored on your device until they expire or are deleted. These help remember preferences between visits.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Nova Hub may use third-party services for analytics and advertising (such as advertising networks).

These third parties may set their own cookies and are governed by their own policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

6. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Nova Hub uses browser localStorage to save user preferences.

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
